Secrets With Strangers

Have you ever wanted to share a secret with someone without them knowing who you are? Are you interested in the secrets of others?

Secrets With Strangers is a new and exciting chat application where you begin by entering a personal secret. The application then pairs you with a random stranger and reveals your secret to them, and their secret is revealed to you.

Now comes the awkward part... You and the stranger are immediately thrown into a chat room to begin discussion of your secrets. The rest is up to you!

If you have any suggestions, we'd love to hear them. Email secretswithstrangers@gmail.com with feedback.

Available on the iPhone

Requirements: Compatible with iPhone and iPod touch. Requires iPhone OS 4.3 or later. WiFi, Edge, or 3G network connection required.
